Our Client

  • Client Name: Hassel Software AS
  • Contact person: Vigbjørn Hassel Vaage

Hassel Software is a Norwegian tech startup created by a company with more than a hundred years experience of selling and distributing cars. Since 2015, we have developed software to digitize traditional dealerships and dealership networks. Our platforms provides everything you need to digitize your existing dealer network. Besides offering your customers with a new customer journey, our systems also provides unparalleled efficiency, uniformity and control of the management of your network.

Purpose, goals and audience

The main goal is to make potential customers interested enough to make contact and ask for a meeting.
Main target audience are potential customers.

Website visitors will primarily be potential customers of the company seeking more information about the company and the product. More specific, these customers range from car national distributors and dealership groups and smaller stand-alone dealers. They can be expected to have at least a basic understanding of the product before arriving on the page. Other users may be potential investors or people who have heard about the product/company and are just curious. However, the main target audience are potential customers.

Images will show a lot of the concept and will be helpful to understand the product. In addition, short and general descriptions should help spark an interest.

Requirements

  • The website must contain a photo gallery with images of the product in use.
  • The website must have a contact form for potential customers to get in touch with me.
  • The website must have a professional look and feel which gives the customer faith in the abilities of the company.
  • The design must look and feel clean.
  • The website must showcase the use and potential benefits of implementing the software solution.
  • The website should make it easier for the potential customer to get to know the product.
  • The website should not be too detailed.
