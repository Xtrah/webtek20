Our Client

  • Client Name: Hassel Software AS
  • Contact person: Vigbjørn Hassel Vaage

Hassel Software is a Norwegian tech startup created by a company with more than a hundred years experience of selling and distributing cars. Since 2015, we have developed software to digitize traditional dealerships and dealership networks. Our clients platforms provides everything their customers need to digitize existing dealer networks, from webshop to showroom. Besides offering their customers with a new customer journey, the systems also provides unparalleled efficiency, uniformity and control of the management of the network.

Purpose, goals and audience

The main goal of this web site is to make potential customers of our client interested enough to make contact and ask for a meeting.
Main target audience are potential customers of the clients product.

Website visitors will primarily be potential customers of the company seeking more information about the company and the product. More specific, these customers range from national car importers and distributers, dealership groups and smaller stand-alone dealers. They can be expected to have at least a basic understanding of the product before arriving on the page. Other users may be potential investors or people who have heard about the product/company and are just curious. However, the main target audience are potential customers.

We plan to deliver on these goals in making the site informative, and present the product our client is selling through different images showing the software in use, text to support what the images is telling. Google maps integration to show the locations of where the product is installed at existing customers, allowing a potential customer to locate a shop or showroom and enabeling the customer to physically interact with the product. This will deliver both our client, and their customers an added benefit and allows the customer to interact with the system in a live environment at their own, pace. Together, these elements will give the web site visitors a firm understanding of the benefits and potential of using the product.

"The main goal of this web site is to make potential customers of our client interested enough to make contact and ask for a meeting."

We feel that this is best achieved through a simpel and concise website consisting of only one page that has different sections downward. this is simple and intuitiv for the customer combined with a sticky navbar, this will give the customer what they were seeking upon visiting the site and hopefully reach out and make contact with out client..

Content of the website

We have not yet decided upon the layout, and want our client to voice an opinion before committing to a design structure. We have agreed to present the design sketch and allow for adjustment in P2. This is a rough outline of what the sites content might look like.

  • Navbar

    • - Sticky navbar linking to fragments/sections down below

  • Welcome section

    • - Big image showing the product, confirming the visitor has arrived at the correct site with text to further enhance the image.

  • About us

    • - Short description about the company, combined with a descriptiv picture.

  • Benefits of the products

    • - Three to five key selling points of the company together with image.

  • Google maps integration

    • - Map showing the location of live installments of the software, allowing the customer to get hands on experiance with the system.

  • Image gallary

    • - Image galery showing the product

  • Our technology

    • - Text describing the clients technology and selling points of the product, to enhance the understanding of how the company is delivering added value to its customers

  • Contact form

    • - A contact form allowing the site to convert interested visitors into meetings, and creating potential customers

Requirements

  • The website must contain a photo gallery with images of the product in use.
  • The website must have a contact form for potential customers to get in touch with me.
  • The website must have a professional look and feel which gives the customer faith in the abilities of the company.
  • The design must look and feel clean.
  • The website must showcase the potential of implementing the software solution, and also showcase existing implementations.
  • The website should make it easier for the potential customer to get to know the product.
  • The website should not be too detailed.
