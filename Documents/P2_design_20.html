Our Client

  • Client Name: Hassel Software AS

Hassel Software is a Norwegian tech startup created by a company with more than a hundred years experience of selling and distributing cars. Since 2015, we have developed software to digitize traditional dealerships and dealership networks. Our platforms provides everything you need to digitize your existing dealer network, from webshop to your showroom. Besides offering your customers with a new customer journey, our systems also provides unparalleled efficiency, uniformity and control of the management of your network.

Purpose, goals and audience

The main goal of this web site is to make potential customers of our client interested enough to make contact and ask for a meeting. Main target audience are potential customers of the clients product.

Website visitors will primarily be potential customers of the company seeking more information about the company and the product. More specific, these customers range from national car importers and distributors, dealership groups and smaller stand-alone dealers. They can be expected to have at least a basic understanding of the product before arriving on the page. Other users may be potential investors or people who have heard about the product/company and are just curious. However, the main target audience are potential customers.

We plan to deliver on these goals in making the site informative and present the product our client is selling through different images showing the software in use with text to support what the images are telling. Google maps integration to show the locations of where the product is installed at existing customers shops or showrooms, allowing a potential customer to locate a shop or showroom and enabling the customer to physically interact with the product. This will deliver both our client, and their customers, an added benefit and allows the customer to interact with the system in a live environment at their own, pace. Together, these elements will give the web site visitors a firm understanding of the benefits and potential of using the product.

"The main goal of this web site is to make potential customers of our client interested enough to make contact and ask for a meeting."

We feel that this is best achieved through a simple and concise website consisting of only one page with has different sections scrolling down. This is simple and intuitive for the customer combined with a sticky navigation bar, which will give the customer what they were seeking upon visiting the site and hopefully reach out and make contact with our client.

Navigation structure

The navigation structure of our website can be described as an all in one page for the product. Our client chose to do it this way because of ease for the customer. The purpose of linking all the sections in one page, is to make it easier for the customers of the website to browse freely through our different services. From all the sections on the website is it possible to access another one (Shown in figure 1).

We chose a clique structure to give our customers as much freedom as possible within the website. On mobile devices and smaller screens, the NAV-bar is accessed by clicking an hamburger-menu-icon which will open a menu for the customer. On a desktop the NAV-bar is fixed to the top of the websites. The function of the NAV-bar is to make different anchors for the different sections in the webpage, so browsing can be done all in one click and without scrolling.
